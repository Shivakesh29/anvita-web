About Us

ANVITA RF & MICROWAVE SYSTEMS PRIVATE LIMITED
We are a leading provider in the design, development, and manufacturing of innovative RF and microwave solutions for complex requirements in telecommunications, aerospace, and defense. Our expertise spans across filters, amplifiers, oscillators, frequency synthesizers, solid-state power amplifiers, T/R modules, exciter receivers, and up/down converters operating up to 18GHz.

We deliver reliable components and custom subsystems, tailoring each solution to our clients’ needs, with a commitment to quality, precision, and next-generation technology. Partner with ANVITA for best-in-class RF and microwave engineering.
